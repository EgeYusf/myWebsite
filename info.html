TRADİNG İNFORMATİON

Before entering this market, it is necessary to understand methods such as fundamental and technical analysis, chart readings, formations, reading the announced balance sheets of companies on the stock exchange, and exchange analysis. What I care about most is the chart of the coin or share. The formation it creates is very important. According to the chart, you can know the average destination, but you can never buy from the bottom and sell from the top. If you have such a thought, you should give it up quickly.
